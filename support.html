Common questions

How do I manage or cancel my VIP subscription?
Open the iPhone Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → ChronosFinance, then manage or cancel. You can also tap Manage subscription inside the app's membership screen.

How do I restore a purchase?
Open the membership screen in the app and tap Restore purchases.

How do I delete my account and data?
In the app, go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your transactions, recurring expenses, income sources, journal entries, and settings. You may also email us to request deletion.

Why does the app ask for Calendar access?
With your permission, ChronosFinance reads your calendar events to forecast income and spending. You can turn this off any time in iOS Settings → Privacy → Calendars.
