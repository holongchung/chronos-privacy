Privacy Policy — ChronosFinance

Last updated: 6 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ChronosFinance (the “App”, “we”, “us”), operated by Ho Long Chung, collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

  • Account information. Your email address (your password is stored securely by our authentication provider and is never visible to us). If you choose Sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier and the email you authorize.
  • Financial information you enter. Transactions (amounts, categories, notes, dates), balances, income sources and rates, budgets, goals, and journal entries. You enter this yourself — the App does not connect to your bank or access bank accounts.
  • Calendar information. With your permission, the App reads events from the calendars you choose (event title, start/end time, and recurrence) to forecast your income and spending. You can revoke calendar access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Purchase information. Subscriptions are processed by Apple. We receive your subscription status but never your payment card details.
  • Diagnostic data. Basic logs needed to run the App. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide the core features: cash-flow forecasting, goal tracking, and spending/income analysis.
  • To generate AI insights (classifying calendar events and producing the VIP “Coach” summaries).
  • To maintain your account and sync your data across your devices.

We do not sell your personal information or use it for advertising.

3. AI processing (Google Gemini)

To classify calendar events and generate insights, the App sends limited data to Google’s Gemini API: event titles and durations, and — for VIP “Coach” summaries — aggregated figures such as period income/spending totals, category breakdowns, and goal amounts. We send only what is needed for the analysis and do not send your name, email, or account identifiers. This data is processed by Google under its Privacy Policy.

4. Service providers

  • Supabase — database and authentication (stores your account and app data). See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Google — Gemini API for AI analysis (see Section 3).
  • Apple — App Store, Sign in with Apple (if used), and subscription billing. See Apple Privacy Policy.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on our providers’ servers, protected by access controls (row-level security, so each user can only access their own data) and encryption in transit (HTTPS). No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can delete your account at any time in Settings → Delete Account, which permanently removes your transactions, recurring expenses, income sources, journal entries, and settings from our servers. You may also contact us to request deletion.

7. International transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside Hong Kong (for example, by Supabase and Google). By using the App you consent to such transfers.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

9. Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data within the App, or by contacting us. Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under applicable law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above.
